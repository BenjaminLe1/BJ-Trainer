First things first

What is Blackjack?

Blackjack (also called "21") is the most popular casino card game in the world — and one of the few where your decisions actually change the odds.

The goal is simple: get a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. Cards 2–10 are worth face value. Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10. Aces are worth 1 or 11.

Hit 21 on your first two cards and that's a Blackjack — an automatic win paying 3:2. With perfect strategy, the house edge drops to just 0.5%.

The skill

What is Card Counting?

Card counting tracks the ratio of high cards to low cards remaining in the shoe. The more high cards, the higher your edge. It's a simple running number — not memorization.

Common myths

  • ❌ "It's illegal"Perfectly legal. Casinos can ask you to leave, but no law is broken.
  • ❌ "You need to be a genius"The math is trivial. Discipline and preparation is the real skill.
  • ❌ "It only works in movies"The MIT Blackjack Team won millions. Real people, real money.

Every card changes your count by +1, 0, or −1. That's the whole Hi-Lo system.
